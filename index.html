SENI

Structured Evaluation Layer for Mobility System Motion Behavior
A framework to analyze consistency between motion behavior representations in ADAS and autonomous mobility systems.

Purpose

SENI is a structured evaluation framework for analyzing vehicle motion behavior in modern mobility systems.

It supports system-level understanding of how motion behavior is represented and evaluated across development and validation environments.

Why It Matters

Validation of mobility systems often relies on isolated testing of components or estimation models.

However, inconsistencies between expected motion behavior and observed vehicle behavior are not always explicitly evaluated at the system level.

SENI addresses this gap through a structured evaluation perspective.

System Context

Modern mobility systems rely on the representation and interpretation of vehicle motion behavior under real-world operating conditions.

Differences may exist between expected and observed behavior due to dynamic environmental factors.

SENI provides a structured perspective for analyzing these differences at a system abstraction level.

Concept Overview

SENI defines a structured approach for:
  • Analyzing vehicle motion behavior at a system abstraction level
  • Evaluating consistency between different representations of motion behavior
  • Supporting validation-oriented analysis in mobility development workflows
  • Comparative evaluation under real-world conditions

Intended Use

  • Vehicle dynamics evaluation studies
  • ADAS and autonomous mobility development workflows
  • Mobility system benchmarking and validation studies
  • Motion behavior consistency research

Key Characteristics

  • System-level evaluation perspective
  • Multi-level abstraction of motion behavior
  • Consistency and comparative analysis focus
  • Independent of implementation-specific methods

Core Principle

SENI focuses on consistency between different representations of vehicle motion behavior rather than implementation-specific methods.

It provides a structured evaluation layer for analyzing system-level consistency in mobility systems.

Important Note

SENI is a conceptual framework for evaluation and analysis.

It does not define or rely on specific hardware configurations, sensor implementations, or algorithmic processing methods.
